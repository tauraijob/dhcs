About Daniella HomeCare

company profile

Daniella homecare solutions was established in September 2016, with a vision to deliver temporary and permanent healthcare recruitment for the public, private and charitable sectors of the community. DHCS Ltd has since expanded into delivering healthcare training; due to the director possessing the skills and knowledge to develop and tailor evidenced based training to care organizations. DHCS Ltd offers a bespoke training service to over 20 care homes in the United Kingdom. Our training differs from most organizations as DHCS Ltd tailors the training to meet the needs of the service users, as well as strategically plans and manages training matrixes to ensure the homes are CQC complaint

Our Services

DHCS LTD VISION AND TRAINING DELIVERY

Building community capacity (Community Training Project) DHCS Ltd is currently delivering short courses in the community to Ipswich Council, Adult Social Care and local churches around meeting the needs of the community. This includes enhancing employability skills for individuals to become placed within the healthcare sector as well as running free dementia education sessions for individuals who are primary carers of dementia sufferers. There is a plethora of specialist courses available to organization’s and we have a great demand for these within the community.

Temporary and Permanent Recruitment

    About

    Daniella Home Care Solutions is a registered company providing, both temporary& permanent recruitment services within different sectors of the care industry. We are a family run business, and deliver a unique blend of innovative high level of care service that is cost effective and tailored to the specific needs of our customers and clients.

    We are very passionate about the services we provide, by ensuring experienced and qualified manpower is supplied to meet all your daily requirements. We currently work closely with local authorities by supplying high standard of service to both our clients in their own homes, residential care settings and extra care housing services. The company is committed to building lasting relationships with our customers and candidates, based on our ethos to deliver an honest, reliable, cost effective and respectful service across board. Our company is regulated by the UK Department of Business, Innovation and Skills (BIS).

    Services

    Our head office is based in Ipswich. We provide staffing solutions holistically tailored exactly to the needs of our clients and customers. Daniella Home Care Solutions specializes in recruiting individuals for domestics, catering, qualified and unqualified social care workforce, we work with people with learning disabilities, mental health issues, young ex- offenders as well as elderly people secretariat and administration and IT, to work as part of the team in different departments of prospective clients all over the UK.

    We offer full and part-time placements on both temporary and permanent basis. Our offices are opened between 9 – 5 pm, Monday to Friday but we also have 24/7 out of hours’ service to ensure constant and continuous availability to clients and candidates, irrespective of the time

    Candidates

    We recruit permanent and temporary staff that covers registered nurses (RGN, RN, RMN, EN), Care workers (Team Leader, Senior Career (NVQ 3, Care Assistants with NVQ 2 or ongoing; Support Workers); Catering (Chef, Cook, Kitchen Assistant); Domestics (Cleaners, Handyman, housekeeper etc.)

    IT (Soft and hardware professionals, Java operatives) Administration (Secretary, Receptionist, Administrative Assistants, Personal Assistants, Clerks). Our candidates are fully trained and experienced. They all have undergone rigorous security checks and have five-year history and eligibility to work in the UK verified. All our candidates have profile cards that are available for clients to view on request.

    Our candidates will come to your establishment carrying their current CRB form, an identification card as well as their profile card indicative of their qualification, training and experience. As a company committed to professional and personal development, all our candidates have current certificates for all mandatory training as well as industry specific qualifications that can enhance the level of their performance in practice. Please feel free to contact us for further information/enquiry about our services.
